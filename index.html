About Me

I am an undergraduate physics and math student at the College of William & Mary, where I am a member of our High Energy Theory Group and Ultra-Cold AMO Physics Laboratory. My current research concerns emergent theories of quantum gravity, 10D supergravity, and novel methods of darkground imaging.

In my free time I play the violin for WMSO and the oud for the Middle East Music Ensemble at the College of William & Mary. I am also an avid amateur astronomer.
